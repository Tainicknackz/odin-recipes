Touchdown Pizza

Description

Touchdown Pizza is the best pre-game treat because it's full of strong flavours and delicious textures. Pepperoni, sausage, bell peppers, onions, and marinara sauce are just some of the hearty toppings on this pizza. The crust is golden and crispy, and the cheese is gooey and melty.

You can drizzle it with tangy barbecue sauce or ranch to make it great for watching your favourite team win.

Ingredients

  • Prebaked pizza crust (such as Boboli)
  • ½ cup Marinara sauce (or barbecue sauce for a smoky flavor)
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup pepperoni slices
  • ½ cup cooked crumbled sausage
  • ¼ cup diced bell peppers (red, green, or yellow)
  • ¼ cup chopped onions
  • 2 tbsp ranch dressing or barbecue sauce (for drizzling)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it inside the oven to heat up.
  2. Lightly flour your work surface and roll out the dough into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Transfer the dough onto a pizza pan or a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  4. Spread the marinara or barbecue sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella and cheddar cheese over the sauce.
  6. Evenly distribute pepperoni slices, crumbled sausage, bell peppers, and onions over the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle a little extra cheese on top for a melty finish.
  8. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Drizzle with ranch dressing or barbecue sauce for extra flavor.
