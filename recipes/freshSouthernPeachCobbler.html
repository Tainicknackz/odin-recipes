Fresh Southern Peach Cobbler

Description

Sweet peaches and warm spices like cinnamon and nutmeg are mixed together in this comforting dish to make a rich and flavourful filling. The topping, which can be a biscuit-like dough or a crispy, buttery crust, bakes to a golden brown colour, making every bite warm and satisfying.

This Southern favourite is great for family get-togethers, summer cookouts, or just a cosy home-cooked treat. It goes well with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 6-8 fresh peaches, peeled and sliced
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (melted)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the sliced peaches with sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, vanilla, lemon juice, and cornstarch.
  3. Stir well to coat the peaches evenly.
  4. Pour the peach mixture into a greased baking dish.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Add the milk and stir until just combined.
  7. Pour in the melted butter and mix lightly.
  8. Pour the batter evenly over the peach filling—it will spread as it bakes.
  9. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the peaches are bubbly.
  10. Let the cobbler cool for about 10 minutes before serving.
