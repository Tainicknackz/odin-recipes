Cheesy Bacon, Ham, and Swiss Tater Tots Poutine

Description

Cheesy Bacon, Ham and Swiss Tater Tots Poutine is a tasty version of the famous Canadian dish that uses crispy tater tots instead of regular fries. This delicious comfort food is packed with crispy bacon, tasty gammon and melted Swiss cheese, all covered in a rich gravy.

Crunchy tater tots mixed with creamy melted cheese, ham and bacon create a tasty and filling dish that’s great for sharing or having as a meal by itself. This poutine is great for a snack on game day or a comforting dinner. It’s sure to amaze!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag (32 oz) frozen tater tots
  • 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • ½ cup cooked and crumbled bacon
  • ½ cup diced ham
  • 2 cups brown gravy (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups beef or chicken broth (for homemade gravy)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and bake the tater tots according to the package instructions until golden and crispy.
  2. Prepare the gravy (if making homemade): Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat, whisk in flour, and cook for 1-2 minutes. Gradually add broth while whisking until smooth. Simmer until thickened, seasoning with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy, then drain on paper towels. Dice the ham into small pieces.
  4. Assemble the dish: Place the crispy tater tots in a serving dish or baking sheet. Sprinkle the shredded Swiss cheese, bacon, and ham evenly over the tots./li>
  5. Pour the hot gravy over the loaded tater tots, ensuring the cheese melts and everything is well coated.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley (if using) and serve immediately while hot and cheesy.
